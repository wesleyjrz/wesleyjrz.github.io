About Me


How do I fell in love with computers

My name is Wesley Júnior and I love computers since I was a kid (I spent most of my time installing malwares on my grandpa's computer... He had to install a Linux distribution for me, because I was curious and kept trying to install every game I saw on the internet). Nevertheless, when I got my own computer I was twelve, and didn't get internet yet, then I had to find a way to have fun, so every day when I got home, I turned on my PC with Windows XP installed, and explored everything I could, it was at those moments that I fell in love with computers. A few years later, I had my first contact with Linux distributions, Linux Mint was my first distribution and I installed it in dual boot with Windows 10. I tried several debian-based distributions, but I didn't like it that much, though when I tried Manjaro Linux I just loved and use it so far.
